## 4. ROI Analysis

### 4.1 Value Proposition (Per 1,000 Employees)

| Benefit | Current State | With MindGuard | Savings |
|---------|---------------|----------------|---------|
| Turnover (mental health) | 5% = 50 | 3.75% = 37.5 | 12.5 employees |
| Replacement cost | $50K/emp | $50K/emp | $625K |
| Absenteeism | 8 days/yr | 6 days/yr | $400K |
| Presenteeism | 20% productivity loss | 15% | $300K |
| Healthcare costs | $1.5M | $1.35M | $150K |
| **Total Annual Savings** | | | **$1.475M** |

| Metric | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Clients (orgs) | 10 | 30 | 75 |
| Avg employees/client | 2,000 | 3,000 | 5,000 |
